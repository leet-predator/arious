A UAE-registered company, Singularious is designed and developed in partnership with the world-leading companies, which is geared towards bringing beneficial planetary-scale changes using the advanced technologies (Spatial Web, Artificial General Intelligence, Blockchain, Robotics, IoT, Metaverse, Digital Twin, Virtual and Augmented Reality). It was founded with initial operations in GCC countries as well as Africa with global aspirations.

In its current portfolio, Singularious has two : main projects:

Firstly, we focused primarily on AI and Blockchain projects in Africa, such as digitalizing the African economy and launching a series of cryptocurrencies backed by natural resources.

Secondly, the creation of the world's first Conscious City; the concept is to create an integrated urban system, organized by intelligent infrastructure, capable of creating holistic value that can be optimized adaptively.

With a Conscious City, you can transcend the failings and shortcomings of Smart Cities. It understands its residents, attends to individual needs with precision, and promotes collective prosperity in a consistent way on an urban scale by means of systemic coordination.

ARIOUS META VISION FOR AFRICA is to systematize and legalize artisanal mining, partnering with governments on the continent to help Artisanal Miners improve their working conditions so that they can increase production and work in a safer environment.
ARIOUS ENERGY is a privately held, East African independent oil and gas company, incorporated and based in the United Arab Emirates.
ARIOUS Investment Group has already established and incorporated a FULL OPERATIVE BANK with a WORLDWIDE MANAGEMENT TEAM with over 30 years of experience
As the world's largest private gem and art collection, Arious serves as a benchmark on a variety of worldwide unique exhibitions.
